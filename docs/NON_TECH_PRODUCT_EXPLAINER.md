# AI Seller Copilot Pakistan - Non-Technical Explainer

## What This Product Does
This is a business assistant for Pakistani sellers and small businesses.
It helps with daily operations:
- Stock tracking
- Customer messaging (Urdu + English)
- Owner alerts
- Payment workflows (JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfer, COD)

## Simple Way to Explain It
"This is like a smart digital manager for your shop. It helps you track stock, talk to customers quickly, and manage payments without extra staff."

## Main Business Value
- Save time on repetitive tasks
- Reduce missed orders and low-stock problems
- Improve customer communication on WhatsApp
- Standardize payment collection flow
- Give owner a clearer daily view of business health

## Who It Is For
- Kiryana / retail shops
- Pharmacies and clinics
- Restaurants and cafes
- Service businesses

## What Is Live Today
- Local web app and API
- Payment flow in sandbox mode for demos
- Seller-focused prompts and workflows for Pakistan market

## What Changes in Live Payment Mode
Sandbox mode is for demo/testing.
Live mode connects actual merchant credentials so real JazzCash/EasyPaisa transactions can run.
