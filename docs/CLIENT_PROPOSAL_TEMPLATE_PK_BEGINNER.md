### Objective
Build and run a practical AI assistant system for daily operations (customer replies, order updates, payment workflow, and reporting) with WhatsApp integration.

## 2) Scope of Work

Included:
- Setup and deployment of web app/API
- WhatsApp Business integration (Meta Cloud API)
- Payment flow setup (JazzCash/EasyPaisa/Bank Transfer/COD as needed)
- Admin training (1-2 sessions)
- Basic monitoring and monthly health checks

Not included unless added:
- ERP/POS deep integration
- Native mobile app
- Complex multilingual bot training pipeline

## 3) Delivery Milestones

1. Kickoff and requirements confirmation (Day 1-2)  
2. System setup + basic flows ready (Day 3-7)  
3. WhatsApp + payment integration (Day 8-12)  
4. UAT and revisions (Day 13-15)  
5. Go-live and handover (Day 16)

## 4) Support SLA

- Working hours support: Mon-Sat, 10 AM to 7 PM PKT
- Critical issues: first response within 2-4 hours
- Normal issues: first response within 24 hours
- Monthly maintenance window: 1 scheduled window

## 5) Pricing (Keep Entry Low So Client Stays)

### Option A: Starter (Best for first-time clients)
- One-time setup: PKR 85,000
- Monthly support/platform fee: PKR 18,000
- Variable usage billed at cost (WhatsApp + AI) with 15% management fee

### Option B: Growth
- One-time setup: PKR 150,000
- Monthly support/platform fee: PKR 35,000
- Variable usage billed at cost with 20% management fee

### Option C: Pro
- One-time setup: PKR 250,000
- Monthly support/platform fee: PKR 60,000
- Variable usage billed at cost with 25% management fee

## 6) Beginner-Friendly Negotiation Strategy

- Start by offering `Option A`.
- Give first-month discount of 10%-15% on support fee only.
- Do not discount variable pass-through costs (WhatsApp/API actual costs).
- Keep contract minimum 3 months so onboarding effort is recovered.

## 7) Cost and Profit Example (Starter)

Example monthly operating cost:
- Cloud hosting + DB + backup: PKR 11,000
- Support effort allocation: PKR 4,000
- Total fixed monthly cost: PKR 15,000

Your price:
- Monthly fixed fee: PKR 18,000
- Fixed profit: PKR 3,000/month
- Plus margin on variable usage (15%)

Note:
- In early projects, prioritize testimonials and retention over high margins.
- Increase pricing after 2-3 successful deployments.

## 8) Payment Terms

- 50% of setup fee in advance
- 50% of setup fee at go-live
- Monthly fee billed at start of each month
- Delay beyond 10 days may pause support

## 9) Contract Notes

- Client owns their business data.
- Third-party service fees are billed separately (Meta/Twilio/OpenAI/cloud).
- Scope changes are handled through change request and revised quote.
