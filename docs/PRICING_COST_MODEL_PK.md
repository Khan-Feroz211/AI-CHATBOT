# Pricing and Cost Model (Pakistan)

Date: 24-Feb-2026  
Purpose: Partner discussion doc for deciding client pricing, cost recovery, and profit targets.

## 1) Assumptions

- Exchange-rate sensitivity exists; keep a 10% buffer in proposals.
- Costs are split into:
  - Fixed monthly costs (hosting, maintenance time, monitoring)
  - Variable usage costs (WhatsApp conversations/messages, AI tokens)
- New freelancer/agencies should prioritize client retention over high margins in first 3-5 clients.

## 2) Monthly Cost Buckets (Per Client)

### A) Fixed costs (estimated ranges)
- Cloud app hosting + DB + backups: PKR 8,000 to 20,000
- Monitoring/logging/alerts: PKR 1,000 to 3,000
- Domain + SSL + misc infra share: PKR 1,000 to 2,500
- Support time allocation (basic): PKR 4,000 to 12,000

Estimated fixed total:
- Lean setup: PKR 14,000 to 20,000
- Stable setup: PKR 20,000 to 37,500

### B) Variable costs (usage-dependent)
- WhatsApp usage (Meta/Twilio): PKR 3,000 to 60,000+
- AI API usage: PKR 500 to 8,000+

Estimated variable total:
- Low usage: PKR 3,500 to 8,000
- Medium usage: PKR 8,000 to 25,000
- High usage: PKR 25,000 to 68,000+

## 3) Beginner-Friendly Client Packages

### Starter (recommended first offer)
- One-time setup: PKR 85,000
- Monthly fixed fee: PKR 18,000
- Variable usage: actual cost + 10% to 15% management fee
- Good for: first-time automation clients

### Growth
- One-time setup: PKR 140,000
- Monthly fixed fee: PKR 32,000
- Variable usage: actual cost + 15% to 20%
- Good for: higher message traffic, more support

### Pro
- One-time setup: PKR 230,000
- Monthly fixed fee: PKR 55,000
- Variable usage: actual cost + 20% to 25%
- Good for: serious operations and tighter SLA

## 4) Profit Scenarios

### Scenario A: Starter client (low usage)
- Revenue:
  - Fixed fee: PKR 18,000
  - Variable pass-through margin (example): PKR 1,200
  - Total monthly revenue: PKR 19,200
- Cost:
  - Fixed infra/support cost: PKR 15,500
  - Variable actual cost: billed to client separately
- Estimated monthly gross profit: PKR 3,700

### Scenario B: Growth client (medium usage)
- Revenue:
  - Fixed fee: PKR 32,000
  - Variable margin (example): PKR 3,500
  - Total monthly revenue: PKR 35,500
- Cost:
  - Fixed infra/support cost: PKR 24,000
- Estimated monthly gross profit: PKR 11,500

### Scenario C: Pro client
- Revenue:
  - Fixed fee: PKR 55,000
  - Variable margin (example): PKR 8,000
  - Total monthly revenue: PKR 63,000
- Cost:
  - Fixed infra/support cost: PKR 36,000
- Estimated monthly gross profit: PKR 27,000

## 5) Discount Policy (To Avoid Client Drop-Off)

- Discount only fixed monthly fee, not pass-through usage costs.
- Safe beginner discounts:
  - First month: 10% off fixed fee
  - Annual prepay: 1 month free out of 12 (effective ~8.3% discount)
- Never discount below your fixed monthly cost floor.

## 6) Contract Guardrails

- Minimum term: 3 months
- Setup payment: 50% advance, 50% at go-live
- Monthly invoice: advance billing at start of month
- Out-of-scope custom work billed separately
- Third-party platform charges billed as actuals

## 7) Internal Decision Sheet (You + Partner)

Decide and freeze these before proposals:
- Final package names and price points
- Minimum acceptable monthly profit per client
- Max discount authority (without partner approval)
- SLA response times for each package
- When to increase prices (for example after first 3 successful clients)

## 8) Recommended Starting Strategy

1. Start with Starter + Growth only for first 2-3 clients.
2. Keep onboarding smooth and over-deliver on communication.
3. Collect testimonials and case results.
4. Increase setup fee by 15% to 25% after proof of success.
