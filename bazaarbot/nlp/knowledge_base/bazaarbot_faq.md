# BazaarBot FAQ — Apka Business Assistant 🇵🇰

## BazaarBot kya hai?
BazaarBot ek AI-powered WhatsApp business assistant hai jo Pakistani chote
dukandar aur retailers ke liye banaya gaya hai. Aap apna stock manage kar sakte
hain, customers ke orders le sakte hain, payments handle kar sakte hain, aur
appointments book kar sakte hain — sab WhatsApp pe!

## Yeh kaise kaam karta hai?
WhatsApp pe "hi" ya "salam" likhein aur main menu aa jata hai. Numbers ya
keywords se koi bhi feature use karein — bilkul asaan.

## Stock kaise check karein?
"1" ya "stock" likhein WhatsApp pe.

## Naya product kaise add karein?
"add product [naam] [quantity] [unit] [price]" format mein likhein.
Misaal: add product Atta 100 kg 65

## Sale kaise record karein?
"sell [product] [qty]" likhein. Stock automatically minus ho jayega.

## Order kaise lein?
"2" ya "order" likhein aur format follow karein.

## Payment info kaise bhejein?
"4" ya "payment" likhein. Aapka EasyPaisa/JazzCash number show hoga.
Settings mein apna number add karein.

## Appointment kaise book karein?
"5" ya "appointment" likhein aur date aur time enter karein.

## Wholesale market kahan dhundhein?
"3" ya "market [city]" likhein. Misaal: market karachi

## Kaun use kar sakta hai?
- Chote retail dukandar (kirana store)
- Jazz aur Zong franchise dealers
- Wholesale merchants
- Service providers (nai, tailor, mechanic, doctor)
- Restaurant aur dhaba owners
- Mobile phone / accessories shop owners
