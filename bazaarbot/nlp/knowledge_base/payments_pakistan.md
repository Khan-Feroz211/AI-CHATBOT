# Pakistan Payment Methods for Business

## EasyPaisa (Telenor)
- Mobile wallet by Telenor Pakistan
- USSD code: *786#  or EasyPaisa app
- Business merchant account available
- Daily limit: Rs 25,000 (basic), Rs 200,000 (biometric verified)
- Fee: 1-2% on cross-network transfers
- How to receive: Customer sends payment to your registered phone number

## JazzCash (Jazz/Mobilink)
- Mobile wallet by Jazz
- USSD code: *786# or JazzCash app
- Business merchant account available
- Daily limit: Rs 25,000 basic, Rs 500,000 with CNIC verification
- Fee: Free within JazzCash, 1.5% to other wallets
- How to receive: Customer sends to your Jazz number

## Bank Transfer
- Popular banks: HBL, MCB, Meezan Bank (Islamic), UBL, Allied Bank
- Use IBAN for large transfers (PKXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX format)
- Interbank transfer: 1-2 business days via NIFT
- Same bank: instant

## Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Most common for e-commerce in Pakistan
- Couriers: TCS, Leopards, M&P offer COD
- Cost: Rs 50-150 per parcel + percentage of COD amount

## Raast (Instant Payments)
- Pakistan's new instant interbank payment system by State Bank
- Link your Raast ID to receive instant payments
- Works with all major banks and wallets
