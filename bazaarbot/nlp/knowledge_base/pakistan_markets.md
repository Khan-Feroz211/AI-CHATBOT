# Pakistan Wholesale Markets Guide

## Karachi Markets
- **Jodia Bazaar**: Pakistan's largest wholesale market. Food, spices, dry fruits,
  grain. M.A. Jinnah Road, Karachi.
- **Saddar Bazaar**: Electronics, household items, mobile phones. Karachi city centre.
- **Empress Market**: Vegetables, meat, spices, everyday items. Saddar, Karachi.
- **Bolton Market**: Wholesale cloth, garments, fabrics. Near Saddar.
- **Shershah**: Used electronics, motors, industrial parts. Karachi.
- **New Sabzi Mandi**: Fresh vegetables and fruit wholesale. Superhighway, Karachi.

## Lahore Markets
- **Anarkali Bazaar**: Clothes, fabric, accessories, handicrafts. Old Lahore.
- **Azam Cloth Market**: Pakistan's largest cloth wholesale market. Lahore.
- **Hall Road**: Electronics, computers, mobile phones, accessories.
- **Brandreth Road**: Hardware, tools, industrial supplies.
- **Icchra Bazaar**: Fabric, stitching, women's clothing.
- **Badami Bagh Mandi**: Fruit and vegetable wholesale. Lahore.

## Islamabad / Rawalpindi Markets
- **Melody Market**: General consumer goods, electronics. Islamabad.
- **Raja Bazaar**: Wholesale general goods, spices. Rawalpindi.
- **I-8 Markaz**: Electronics, hardware.
- **Bara Kahu**: Vegetables, meat.

## Faisalabad Markets
- **Katchery Bazaar**: Cloth, fabric, yarn. Pakistan's textile hub.
- **Clock Tower Bazaar**: General wholesale goods.

## Online Wholesale Platforms
- Daraz.pk — largest e-commerce platform in Pakistan
- OLX.com.pk — buy/sell new and used goods
- Alibaba.com — import goods from China
- TradeKey.com — Pakistani B2B directory

## Tips for Buying Wholesale
1. Always compare prices from at least 3 suppliers
2. Ask for bulk discount on qty > 100 units
3. Verify supplier reputation (ask for references)
4. Get written receipt / pakka bill
5. For food items check expiry date and halal certification
